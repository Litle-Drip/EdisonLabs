Company Structure

Legal Entity (Parent Company)

Edison Labs LLC

Products

The Juice — a peer-to-peer, on-chain application developed and operated by Edison Labs LLC.

Each product may have its own brand, interface, and domain, while remaining under a single, unified company structure.

The Juice

The Juice is the first product built by Edison Labs LLC.

It is an experimental, blockchain-based application that enables users to interact directly with smart contracts for peer-to-peer outcomes. The Juice operates as a standalone product with its own interface and disclosures.

The Juice is a product of Edison Labs LLC.

How We Operate

  • One legal entity: Edison Labs LLC
  • Multiple products over time
  • No custodial control of user assets
  • Products are experimental by design
  • Clear separation between company and product branding

This structure allows Edison Labs to responsibly build, test, and iterate on new tools without fragmenting ownership or forming unnecessary entities.

Philosophy

Edison Labs is built around a simple idea:

Good software studios should experiment quickly, ship transparently, and remain structurally sound.

We focus on:

  • clean architecture
  • minimal intermediaries
  • clear product boundaries
  • long-term flexibility
